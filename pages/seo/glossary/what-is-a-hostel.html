Hostel

Definition: A hostel is a larger dormitory-style shared accommodation facility, typically offering basic amenities at lower costs than PGs, commonly used by students and budget travellers.

Detailed Explanation

Hostels differ from PGs in scale and management style. While PGs offer a home-like setting with 10-30 residents, hostels can house hundreds of people in dormitory-style rooms. College hostels are attached to educational institutions, while private hostels operate independently. Modern hostels in India have evolved to offer better amenities, but PGs generally provide more personalised service, better food quality, and a closer community feel.

Key Points to Know

  • Larger scale than PGs (50-500+ residents)
  • Dormitory-style rooms with shared facilities
  • Usually cheaper than PGs
  • Less personalised management
  • College hostels have curfew and strict rules
