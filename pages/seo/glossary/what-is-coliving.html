Coliving Space

Definition: Coliving is a modern shared living concept where residents get private bedrooms with shared community spaces like kitchens, lounges, and coworking areas, often managed by tech-enabled platforms.

Detailed Explanation

Coliving has emerged as a premium alternative to traditional PGs, targeting millennials and young professionals. Unlike PGs, coliving spaces are typically managed by startups (like Stanza Living, Zolo) and feature app-based room management, community events, and modern interiors. While coliving offers a premium experience, it comes at a higher price point. For students and budget-conscious residents, well-managed PGs like Kangaroo House offer similar amenities at more affordable rates.

Key Points to Know

  • Modern, tech-enabled accommodation model
  • Private rooms + shared community spaces
  • Managed by organised startups
  • Higher price than traditional PGs
  • App-based services and community events

Frequently Asked Questions

How is coliving different from PG?

Coliving spaces are typically more expensive, tech-enabled, and target young professionals. PGs are more affordable, home-like, and focus on students. The core services (furnished rooms, meals, WiFi) are similar.

Is coliving worth the extra cost?

If you value modern interiors, community events, and app-based management, coliving can be worth it. However, good PGs offer comparable amenities at 30-40% lower cost.
